Our Team TUBITECH

TUBITECH 9694 is an FRC team affiliated with TÜBİTAK Science High School, established in the 2023-2024 season. As a young yet ambitious team, we are committed to pushing the boundaries of technology and innovation while upholding the core values of FIRST. Our journey to the FRC World Championship reinforced our belief in the power of this global community. Witnessing the diversity, collaboration, and shared passion for robotics on an international stage reminded us of the true purpose behind our efforts. We take pride in representing our country, school, sponsors, and the Fikret Yüksel Foundation, the Turkish pillar of FRC, at the highest level.
