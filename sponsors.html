Our Sponsors

At TUBITECH 9694, our success is made possible by the incredible support of our sponsors, who provide both technical and financial assistance. Every part of our robot carries the mark of our material sponsors, enabling us to bring advanced engineering solutions to life. Our financial sponsors, on the other hand, are not just supporting a team—they are investing in a vision. Their contributions allow us to represent our country on international platforms, introduce more young minds to STEAM, and spread the spirit of FIRST. Without their support, this journey would be far more challenging.
