OUR DEVELOPMENT

"FOR A GREENER FUTURE"

Our Projects

Our projects are designed to inspire and engage a diverse range of participants through science, technology, and creativity. Ourproject empowers our members, foster a collaborative environment, and expands our reach to create a lasting impact in the robotics community and beyond. Our goal is to empower individuals, promote STEM education, and support the growth and development of future innovators.

Art Projects
1) NFT Collection

A collection of 1000 NFTs has been created using our mascot, the deer.

2) Team Song

A music track titled 'Beware of Deer,' composed by our teammates and using sounds from the FRC competition, has been produced.

3) Art Piece of Deer

A deer painting has been created using materials that have turned into waste during the scientific process.

4) Re-Grow Beads

Bracelets containing seeds have been made from paper waste. The children have also painted these bracelets.

5) Board Game

A board game containing questions from science, art, and safety fields has been produced to engage the children at LÖSEV and instill a love of science in them.

6) Safety Animation and FRC Game

To draw more attention to safety issues, a Safety Animation has been produced. Also to attract more people to the world of FIRST, a computer-based FRC game has been created.

Our Gender Equality Projects
1) Star Girls of Science

In the project titled 'Star Girls of Science,' supported by Emine MANDAL, the wife of the TÜBİTAK president and an academic, 8th-grade students receive training in LGS, scientific olympiads, and project development. Additionally, they receive training in art and FRC. The project has reached over 6,000 girls.

2) Women'S TEAM

In the project titled 'Women’s TEAM,' followed by the TÜBİTAK president, interviews have been conducted with female researchers at TÜBİTAK about being a woman in the field of science, and a magazine has been published. The magazine has been distributed to the female students at the schools we visited.

Our Technical Projects
1) 2204-A and Polar Symposium

Our team members have participated in the Polar Symposium and the 2204-A Inter-High School Research Projects Competition with their scientific projects.

2) Cultural Promotion

Meetings have been held to promote both Turkish culture and Istanbul to the teams coming from abroad for the competition.

Trainings We Provided
1) Meet the Deer

Within the scope of the Meet the Deer project, detailed FRC training has been provided to the students and teachers at our school. This has ensured the sustainability of the team.

2) Mentorship for Local Schools

Local schools have been visited, reaching over 3,000 people. A mentorship network has been established for FIRST and other competitions.

3) STEAM Library

A library consisting of art and science magazines and books has been established at a school in our district.

4) FRC Tech Talks

In the project titled 'FRC Tech Talks,' interviews have been conducted with FRC alumni to create a comprehensive Turkish resource on FRC.

5) FRCGPT

An artificial intelligence model has been created to answer questions about this year's game to assist the teams.

6) Expanding FRC Outreach

To ensure FRC reaches everyone, science and art activities have been conducted with students with intellectual disabilities, and FIRST has been introduced to them.

7) VEX Team Formation and Mentorship

As a result of the training provided, a VEX team has been established at our school. Active mentorship is being provided to them.

8) The Robot in Your Dreams

An event was organized for elementary school children to design their dream robots. Some of these designs were used as pawns in the game we created.
