Our Team TUBITECH

Founded in May 2023, our FRC team “TUBITECH” consists of 36 students and mentors for the 2024-2025 season. Our team members are carefully selected individuals with exceptional talents and entrepreneurial spirit. During the 2023-2024 season, we won the Marmara Regional Finals, securing the “Winner” award, which earned us the opportunity to represent our country, school, and sponsors in the United States. TÜBİTAK Science High School, established in the 2021-2022 academic year, admits students who are in the top 1% based on their LGS results and undergo a special aptitude test. Our school is renowned for its academic excellence, attracting gifted and talented students across Turkey. Located in the TÜBİTAK Gebze Campus, which hosts some of the leading research centers in the world, TÜBİTAK Science High School has participated in various national and international competitions, including TEKNOFEST, TÜBİTAK 2204 A/C/D High School Students Research Projects Competition, ERASMUS+, and eTwinning, achieving numerous successes. Our school collaborates on joint projects and initiatives with prestigious institutions such as Koç University, Istanbul Technical University, Ankara Science High School, PMI Turkey, the Turkish Debate and Oratory Association, and the Turkish Intelligence Foundation, among others.
